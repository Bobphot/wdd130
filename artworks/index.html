How long will my artwork last?

All of the artwork we sell on our website are considered archival, which means that the pigments used will last 50-100 years without fading. Of course, there are other factors that can reduce the life of your print. For example, where you display your artwork can have an affect on how long it will last. Will it be in direct sunlight half of the day? Is it in a place where it is exposed to chemicals or excessive humidity? These are some of the things to consider when you decide to buy art.
