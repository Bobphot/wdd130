Purpose

Audience

People who would like to order art online and are wondering what the many choices are. We will focus on the 5 options available in our storefront.

Content

Home page

Welcome to our fine art page. We have found that there are a lot of questions out there about artwork, how to choose it, and how it will need to be displayed. Here we hope to answer all of your questions about the different media.

Images for the Home page (See Wireframe for pictures)

Canvas Artwork

Canvas Prints are the traditional selection for fine art. We use archival pigments and canvas and all of our products. There are many ways that you can purchase canvas artwork for your home. We have framed canvas prints. These range in sizes from 12x18 - 40"x80". Another canvas option are gallery wraps. These are a popular and simple way to hang up fine artwork without having to decide on a frame.

Images for the Page 2 (See Wireframe for pictures)

Page 3

Paper, Metal and Acrylic Prints. We are pleased to offer the latest high definition metal and acrylic prints. The colors and images are so vibrant that the almost look 3 dimensional. The metal prints sit off the wall .75", while the acrylic are 1.5" off the wall. If you can't decide, we also offer regular paper prints. They are easy to use or frame by yourself.
