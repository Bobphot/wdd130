CANVAS ART CHOICES

Canvas Wraps

Canvas Wrap – traditionally called gallery wraps, the canvas is printed to appear on the sides of the prints, and the canvas is then stretched around a wood frame before it is stapled n the back. The canvas wrap hangs on directly on the wall, and do not require a frame. The ones that we provide are about 1 3/8” thick, but gallery wraps can vary from 5/8” thick to 3” thick. Depending on the size, the smaller ones come with a hanger attachment, and the larger ones with a double hanger. If you would like to purchase a gallery wrap that you are planning on framing someday, we recommend asking for the 5/8” thickness. This is the traditional thickness that we use for our regularly stretched canvas.

Framed Canvas

This is a timeless way to display quality artwork. We have several frames choices available, however, if you would prefer a different frame, we recommend ordering a loose canvas print that you can take to your local frame who will have dozens of choices. All framed artwork 12x18 and larger come with a wire hanger on the back..

Float Frame on Canvas Wraps

Float frame on Canvas Wrap – We are excited about this new product. It is a combination canvas wrap and framed item. The canvas wrap has a thin frame, maybe 3/8” thick, that sits about the same height as the canvas around the gallery wrap, with a small amount of space in between. These come with the same hangers that are on the canvas wrap. Traditional frame colors are available, white, brown, silver, gold, and black.

Acrylic Prints

Similar to metal, we have a new acrylic product . A photograph is created and then the image is sealed clear of acrylic. They sit off the wall about an 11/2”, and look very similar to the metal prints in vibrance and presentation.
